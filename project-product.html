The Project

The early development of this product will be focused on creating a sizable database and assisting users in applying AI to evaluate and classify food photographs for the detection of potential allergens. In order to enhance the entire experience for consumers when purchasing and consuming food, the subsequent stages of this product will be concentrated on community development and partnership with other businesses.

The application's user experience was our initial consideration. The goal was to create a simple, feature-rich software with an intuitive interface. We therefore made it our first priority to choose a welcoming primary color scheme for our application.
