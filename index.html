About

Our goal is to help users avoid significant allergic reactions while also learning more about themselves and allergies in general. We intend to create an app that is simple to use and includes a profile page, a scanning interface for identification, and a database of allergens. We provide users with an app which includes a pretrained neural network model designed to identify allergens. The app will also educate users on allergic reactions and let them share their experiences with the community.
